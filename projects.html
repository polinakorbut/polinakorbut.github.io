Basketball

Basketball has been the driving force behind my life since 5th grade, and I can't imagine living without it. From playing in Ukraine to becoming a member of the LCC Moose basketball team in Lithuania, I've always pursued my passion for the sport.

It's not just fun and social, but it also helps to develop strong teamwork skills, which are essential for success both on and off the court.

Whether I'm playing with friends or in a league, basketball always brings me joy and a sense of accomplishment.

If you want to see more, you can follow my basketball profile on Instagram: korbut.basketball

Reading

Reading is one of my favorite hobbies. I love immersing myself in a good book and getting lost in the world the author has created.

Fiction and fantasy are my go-to genres, as they transport me to other realms and allow me to escape reality for a while. There's something magical about getting lost in a story and feeling like you're a part of it.

One of my favorite authors is Tahereh Mafi. I have read many of her books, and she always does a great job with writing. The characters are dynamic, and the world-building is genuinely immersive.

Currently, I am reading her book This Woven Kingdom. I highly recommend this book for its exceptional writing style!

Hiking

I enjoy hiking as a hobby, especially in the Ukrainian Carpathians. There's just something so refreshing about being out in nature and getting some exercise.

I particularly love going with my family, as it's always a special experience. And of course, the stunning scenery of the Carpathian Mountains never fails to take my breath away.

This summer, we hiked Petros Mountain and it was an amazing experience! The view from the top was breathtaking.
